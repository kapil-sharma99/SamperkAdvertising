Achievement

Our achievements in this 30 years

In 1981 Samperk Advertising introduced the concept of cinema Advertisemnt for Rajhans. It was multi-star cast ad. film starring Sriram Panda, Tripura Mishra, Swarup Nayak, Niharika Dash, Jaya Biswas and Deepa Sahoo. This advertisement was released from Mumbai.

In 1994 Samperk Advertising collaborated with Doordarshan and released first T.V. commercial advertisement in 18 septemper 1994 for our esteemed client Chandra Oil Expellers for their product Kalpak coconut oil. Actor Uttam Mohanty and actress Aparajita Mohanty were starred in this advertisement. Doordarshan started T.V. commericals with our agency.

In 1882 our agency Samperk Advertisemnts introduced first musical sponsored serial in All India Radio Cuttack named as Rajhans Sangeet Mahal. It was an entertainment program which contains music and a tinge of comedy.

We introduced the first Audio Magazine named as Film Bazar in year 1992. It was a serial of 52 episodes which on air every Sunday. It contains rarest songs and inteview of most famous singers and Music producers. It was released in All India Radio.

Our agency Samperk Advertising Organized the VAT campaign in 2 phases(year 2003-04 and 2005-06) to creating awareness among public and teach them about Value Added Tax. It was in collaboration with DFID India(Department for International Development). We managed to handle this program in all sort pf media. Odisha was the first state to implemnt Value Added Tax.

We create projects in every genre. More than 40 Ad. films, more than 200 T.V. commercials, more than 30 documentary films which contains legendary documentaries of personality like Biju Patnaik. All over we have successfully delivered around 500 projects with best feedbacks.
