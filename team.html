Pradeep Mohanty

Managing Director

Highly experienced and visionary person having print and electronic media journalism background since 1981. Being the managing and creative director of agency, he always thinks of innovative and futuristic ideas to create impact on the target class. He was the key person for VAT campaign in the state of Odisha for DFID during 2003-04 & 2005-06.

Produced and written many Radio programs & sponsored serials for several organizations and film production houses. Conceptualized and Directed a wide range of Ad.films, Tv commercials for various organizations of government and private sectors. Produced and directed a value based Tv serial titled "Samparka doro" for 36 episodes being telecast in Doordarshan (2002-2003).
Produced and directed a consumer awareness Tv serial called " Jagi Utho"for more than 300 episodes being telecast in Doordarshan and Kalinga Tv from 2003 to 2019. Further to these, i have written and directed many documentary films like Mahanayak Biju Pattnaik, Musical legend Akshaya Mohanty, Dr. Radhanath Rath, Professor Writer Sarat Mohanty etc. Besides these, i write contents and edit news letters, exclusive books, souvenirs etc for different clients and occasions.
