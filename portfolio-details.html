Jagi Utho(Awake)

  • Category: Social Awareness
  • Client: Odisha Government
  • Project date: March, 2016

Project Details

A social awareness weekly Video Magazine of 15 minutes duration on consumerism with a speical focus on VAT (Value Added Tax) as well many essential topic like Right to Information (RTI), Road Safety, Electricity, Education, Health & Hygiene, National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) etc. being telecated on every Sunday and Monday at 7:15 p.m. on DD-1 and DD-6 for a period of 3 years in 3 phases.
